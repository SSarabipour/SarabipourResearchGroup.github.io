Welcome to the Vera-Licona Lab

Our research is at the intersection of computational systems biology and systems medicine, mathematical biology, and bioinformatics. We work on the design, software development, and application of mathematical algorithms to model, simulate, and control biological systems. We place a particular emphasis on methods combining multi-omics profiles for data-driven gene regulatory networks and multi-scale Boolean mathematical modeling construction, analysis, and control.

Previous Next

In molecular biology and systems genomics, we are interested in developing and applying tools for cell fate reprogramming. The application areas we are focused on include Immunology, Aging, and Cancer research (see Research).

We are located at the Center for Quantitative Medicine, which is part of the Center for Cell Analysis & Modeling.

We are always looking for passionate students at diverse levels and Postdocs to join the team(more info)!
